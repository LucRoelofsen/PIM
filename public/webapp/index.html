The Rijksmuseum needs your help with digitizing physical documents. With only a limited amount of resources, this process will take a lot of time. By using crowdsourcing, everyone can contribute to this process.

Going digital. Powered by crowdsourcing.

Helping the Rijksmuseum is simple. Once you have registered, you will be shown photos or scans of documents which need to be digitized. Simply write what you see in the input form and submit your transcription.

Earn points. Redeem awesome rewards.

For every document you help us transcribe, you will earn points which you can redeem in our shop. You can redeem awesome rewards, such as official Rijksmuseum merchandise or discount at your favourite stores.

Frequently Asked Questions

RijksCrowd is an crowdsource initiative of 3 students at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. We'd love to tell you more about our project!

General questions

The Rijksmuseum has a large amount of physical documentation which they want to digitize. If they would do this manually on their own, it would be a very long and expensive process. By introducting RijksCrowd, a crowdsourcing solution, anyone can contribute to this digitization process!

When you are transcribing, you will be shown a scanned document of the Rijksmuseum archive. Depending on the task, we may ask you to copy what you see on the document onto our system. We may also ask you to classify documents by submitting metadata (such as dates, titles and so on).

For every document you help us transcribe, you will earn points which you can redeem in our shop. You can redeem awesome rewards, such as official Rijksmuseum merchandise or discount at your favourite stores.

Privacy and account

Your data related to submissions will be used by the Rijksmuseum. All other personal information will never be shared with third parties.

If for any reason you would like to delete your account, you can contact us per mail. We will handle the rest.

You can request a new password on this page. No access to your email address? Send us a mail, we will be trying to help you out.
